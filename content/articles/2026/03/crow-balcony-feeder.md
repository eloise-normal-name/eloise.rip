I used a sewing needle to run a piece of thread through a container of metmarket cashews, and tied it around my balcony railing. I liked seeing LB try to take it 😸. 

It took them almost a week, but today I saw the container had less food in it. I need to remember to clean it out though! I think it will work better if I put a different container inside the secured one that I can remove and wash. I also want a way to provide water for sippies and dippies

Now I won't create a huge mess underneath from sloppy crow friends. I saw boogie get some snacks too, they were still quite timid though. GB let me get so close 🥹
